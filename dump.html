Project 1 - Interface Design (IAT 334) - A3: Interactive Prototype

Project Description: Create with Origami studio, Supernova or other means to create a high-fidelity, multilinear interactive prototype of your interactive system. At a minimum, this prototype should include every step and interaction of the main features of your application.

In addition, you should endeavor to complete as many other features of your application as you can in order to have a broad range of interactions and user flows available for the subsequent user testing stage. Your prototype will be evaluated based on design principles and components of usability you learn in the class. We began by deciding what our design domain was - high school teachers. We then researched about the, what are common goals of people the domain, what are some common activities, what are some of the technologies (including sensors), locations and situations that enable you to design a system, what challenges or opportunities in this context were interesting to our group. We then came up with 3 initial ideas for interactive systems that respond to these challenges/opportunities. Next we did some more research and created sketches of our best idea from our initial 3. Following that we designed in Illustrator, 3 possible interfaces/layouts that our application could use and finalized features. Then we begin creating a prototype using Origami Studio. ....
